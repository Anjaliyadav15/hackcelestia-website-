About us!!

Hack On Hills aims to build an environment of collaborative growth amongst all the participants regardless of their gender, caste, creed, sexual preference, socioeconomic background or cultural belief. Hack On Hills is open to everyone with a crazy idea and a will to implement it into a working prototype.

Hack On Hills has always been supportive for women participation in the event, we have tried our level best to make sure that female candidates get a fair chance to put-forth their ideas and showcase their experience and talent.

For this, we have introduced separate prizes for best all women team and reserving some portions of seats for womens during hackathon.

See our Code of Conduct Here.

GUIDELINES
  • Open to all full-time students regardless of their specialization.
  • Teams must comprise of 4-5 members. Each team must consist of at least 1 female participant as a compulsion.
  • Teams with members from different backgrounds are welcome.
  • Teams with members from different years are allowed.
  • Each participant can be part of only one team.
  • There are no limits on the number of participating teams per institute.
  • Intercollege teams are not allowed.

    FAQs
    Is this hackathon an online event or offline?
    Hackcelestia is a offline event and as a participant the whole team needs to be present at Banasthali Vidyapith Campus.
    Does this hackathon have any participation fees for students?
    No. The hackathon is free of cost for any student from any college all over India.
    What are all the restrictions on Team formations for the event?
    A team can have at most 5 members and minimum of 4 members with atleast one female member from the same college.
    Can i participate with other college teams?
    No, teams should have members from the same college itself. Intercollege teams are not allowed.
    What are the perks and benefits of participating in this hackathon?
    Hackathons provide hands on experience with the experts from different field working on the projects like Blockchain, AI/ML, Machine Learning, etc. This will help to give a head start if any one want to make project on the field. Along with that the participant will be provided with cool swags, stickers and refreshments to keep them up.
    What are the Accommodation Facilities for the participants from outside the College?
    Participant will be provided with accommodation and food in the Guest House of the College during the Hackathon Period.
    How to reach Banasthali Vidyapith for the event?
    The nearest airport to Banasthali Vidyapith is Jaipur Airport (JAI) which is 50.2 km away from the campus.From Jaipur you can easily find cabs or buses from Sindhi Camp(Bus stand) to reach our campus.The nearest Railway Station is Niwai Station which is 5 km away from the campus.
